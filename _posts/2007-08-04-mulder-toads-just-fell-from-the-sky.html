--- layout: post title: Mulder, toads just fell from the sky! --- ... Or how the Moonlight assemblies are created.

Silverlight, as its Mono implementation Moonlight, contains a small subset of what developers can have access to in the traditional versions of the .net and Mono framework.

These days, my work is focused on building the «base class libraries» for Moonlight. They are the reduced assemblies that the Moonlight plugin will use. We've affectionately named them the 2.1 assemblies, as it's the version of the mscorlib.
