DotNetGuru Symposium, Episod III 26 Oct 2005


Last monday I attended with more than 600 persons the third edition of the DotNetGuru Symposium. Last year I did a very brief Mono presentation. This edition was in the DotNetGuru tradition focused on high quality software development. They talked about n-tiers patterns, software industrialization, Smart clients, and then DSL. The nice and short sessions "after lunch", to help people digest, were presented by my friends Sébastien Ros on the implementation of Linq in his next gen ORM tool and Thomas Gil.

The apogee of the event was the Q&A with Bill Gates. I was five meters in front of the richest man of the world. I decided not to be impressed and I looked at him with my look "I've already won", slightly the one you could use with girls, but with nuances of threat. And you know what ? He didn't look scared...
